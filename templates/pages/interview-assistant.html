Generate clear, confident STAR-format responses for UK Civil Service interviews — instantly and for free.

Whether you're applying for SEO, G7, or other grades, the Civil Service interview process revolves around behavioural questions. Our assistant uses AI to help you structure your experience using the STAR method:

  • Situation: What was the background or context?
  • Task: What were you trying to achieve?
  • Action: What did you specifically do?
  • Result: What was the outcome or impact?

Just enter the behaviour (e.g. “Leadership”), a short scenario prompt, and a few lines about your experience — and the assistant will write the rest.
